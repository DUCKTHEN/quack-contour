# Privacy

Quack Contour is intended to run locally on your own machine.

- Imported model files are read from your local browser/server session.
- The app does not upload your OBJ/FBX data to the project author.
- The repository does not store your imported models.
- Generated output is written locally to `outputs/`.
- Generated output may contain measurements or shapes derived from imported model data, so treat it according to the model owner's permission.
- Temporary uploads are written locally to `uploads/`.

Model data remains the responsibility and property of the person or organization that owns it. Do not publish private, client, commercial, or paid model assets unless you have permission.
